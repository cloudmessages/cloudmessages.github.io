# Motivation

Many organisations already use commands, queries, and events. CloudEvents gives the industry a widely recognised cloud-native envelope standard for facts that already happened, but the surrounding message families are often left informal.

Standardising commands and queries can improve clarity and interoperability, especially in event modelling, CQRS, DDD, and message-oriented organisations.

With CloudEvents, CloudCommands, and CloudQueries, organisations can normalise all major message types within high-velocity, fault-tolerant, scalable, message-oriented systems.
